Beacon: Bank Robber

Beacon Richards is out to get rich. Like many in America's West, he is disconcerned with what it takes to get to the top. If you intend to leave your payday checks unattended, beware. This outlaw intends to use your money to buy new treats.

Beacon: Puppy in Contempt

Beacon is a dangerous fiend. Rather than wear a suit and tie when the gavel calls, Beacon doesn't even bother to show up. Parking tickets: eaten. Jail bonds: never posted. Civil judges: out of luck. Beacon is unafraid and on the run.

Beacon: Hiding in Plain Sight

Beacon, a dodgy dog, lives under the radar. Whether it is in his neighbor's garden or under his parents' bed, Beacon has a certain knack of finding places he isn't suspected-- securing the space needed to plot his next evil operation.
